Muralidharan - Article: Generational changes

Change is inevitable and will go on repeating. Most of the changes are not perceptible, only on hindsight will we realise them.

Maruti 800 & Maruti Van which replaced Ambies & Premier Padminies got themselves displaced. Scooters were replaced by Activas. Filament bulbs got replaced by CFL & then LED. Mosaic tiles vanished & Vitrified tiles became the usual norm. Window ACs became extinct only to be replaced by Split ACs. Landline to mobile phones, cheques to atm cards to net banking. LPG Gas cylinders to Pipe Gas. Floppy discs to CDs to DVDs to pen drives to cloud storage. Pagers to mobile phones (Nokia) to smart phones (iPhone and OnePlus) to smart watches to smart finger rings. Doordarshan to multiple channels, 5 minute cartoon space before chitrahaar to full time cartoon channels to online streaming. Photograph negative to digital. Social circles are becoming more and more virtual as each day passes.

The biggest generation shift was in our perception, a shift from physical to virtual.
