Rohit - Learn Japanese words

Expressions

  1. Arigatou = Thank you
  2. Sumimasen = Excuse me / I'm sorry
  3. Beautiful = ki-re-i

Words

  1. Toilet = toire
  2. Train station = eki
  3. Hotel = hoteru
  4. Convenient store = konbini
  5. Chopsticks = Ha-shi (low tone in shi)
  6. Bridge = Ha-shi (high tone in shi)

Relationships

  1. Grandmom = Oba-a-san
  2. Aunt = Oba-san

Sentences

  1. I ate apple = Watashi(I) ringo(apple) tabemashita(ate)
  2. Where is Toilet = toire wa doko desu ka ... wa(append to subject) doko(where) desu(polite word, 'is' in this case) ka(ending it as a question)
  3. Where is Tokyo station = Tokyo eki wa doko desu ka
